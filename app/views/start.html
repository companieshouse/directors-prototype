Use this service to remove (terminate the appointment of) a director of a limited company on the Companies House register, including corporate directors.

What a corporate director is
This is a company, firm, or any other kind of corporate body that is appointed as the director of another company.
For example, COMPANY 1 LIMITED could be the corporate director of COMPANY 2 LIMITED.

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • the company number
  • the company authentication code
  • the date the director was removed from the company

Use this service to update PSC information for limited companies on the Companies House register.

In this service, you can:

  • add a new PSC
  • update an existing PSC's details
  • cease a PSC
  • add or remove a PSC statement

Before you start

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • the company number
  • the company authentication code

To add a new PSC, you'll need to tell us the PSC's:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • nationality
  • correspondence address and home address
  • natures of control
  • notification date and the date the company's PSC register was updated with this information
{{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "What a PSC register is", text: "Companies are required to keep a PSC register. It must include the details of all individuals or companies that have significant control over the company." }) }}

You'll also need to tell us whether the PSC has ever applied to protect personal information at Companies House.

Use this service to update director information for limited companies on the Companies House register.

In this service, you can:

  • appoint a new director
  • update an existing director's details
  • remove a director

Who this service is for

For now, you can only appoint, update or remove a director who is a person in this service. This is sometimes known as a 'natural person' director.

For corporate directors, you'll need to use our WebFiling service or file a Companies House form.

What is a corporate director?
This is a company, firm, or any other kind of corporate body that is appointed as the director of another company.

For example, COMPANY 1 LIMITED could be the corporate director of COMPANY 2 LIMITED.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • the company number
  • the authentication code

To appoint a director, you'll need their:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • nationality
  • occupation
  • correspondence address and home address
  • date of appointment

You'll also need to tell us whether the director has ever applied to protect personal information at Companies House.

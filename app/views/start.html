Use this service to update PSC information for limited companies on the Companies House register.

In this service, you can:

  • appoint a new PSC
  • update an existing PSC's details
  • remove a PSC

Before you start

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • the company number
  • the company authentication code

To appoint a PSC, you'll need their:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • nationality
  • occupation (this is optional)
  • correspondence address and home address
  • date of appointment

You'll also need to tell us whether the PSC has ever applied to protect personal information at Companies House.

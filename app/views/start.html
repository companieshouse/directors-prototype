Use this service to appoint, update or remove (terminate the appointment of) a director of a limited company.

You can:

  • appoint a new director – for now, you can only appoint a director who is a person
  • update an existing director's details – for now, you can only update a director who is a person
  • remove a director – you can remove a director who is a person or a corporate director

To appoint or update a corporate director, you’ll need to use our WebFiling service or file a Companies House form.

What is a corporate director?
This is a company, firm, or any other kind of corporate body that is appointed as the director of another company.

For example, COMPANY 1 LIMITED could be the corporate director of COMPANY 2 LIMITED.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • a Companies House account - we'll ask you to sign in or create one
  • the company number
  • the company authentication code

To appoint a director, you'll need their:

  • name
  • previous names used for business purposes in the past 20 years (if relevant)
  • date of birth
  • nationality
  • occupation (optional)
  • correspondence address and home address
  • date of appointment

You'll also need to tell us whether the director has ever applied to protect personal information at Companies House.

For your security, we’ll sign you out if you do not use the service for 60 minutes. You cannot save your answers.

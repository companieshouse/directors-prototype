Use this service to update director information for limited companies on the Companies House register.

In this service, you can:

  • appoint a new director
  • update an existing director's details
  • remove a director

For now, you cannot appoint, update or remove corporate directors in this service. You can make these changes in WebFiling or by filing a Companies House form.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • the company number
  • the company authentication code

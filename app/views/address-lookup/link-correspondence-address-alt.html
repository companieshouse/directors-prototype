Future changes to addresses

If your registered office address changes in the future, we can apply this change to your correspondence address as well.

This means:

  • when you tell us the company's registered office address has changed, you will not have to tell us your correspondence address has also changed
  • your correspondence address will automatically change to the same address as the registered office address

What information we'll show on the public online register

We will show the country the director lives in on the public Companies House register. We will not show the whole address, unless it's also used for the company's registered office address or the director's correspondence address.

My correspondence address is the same as my home address
We will not show your home address on the public register. However, if you use your home address as your correspondence address, we will show this on the public register.

If you run your company from home and do not want your home address available to the public, there is a list of agents who can provide registered office services (opens in new tab).

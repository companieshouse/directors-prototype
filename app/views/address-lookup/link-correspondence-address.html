Updating your correspondence address

If your registered office address moves to another location in the future, we can update the correspondence address to the same address, at the same time.

This means:

  • when you tell us the company's registered office address has changed, you will not have to tell us your correspondence address has also changed
  • we'll update your correspondence address automatically for you

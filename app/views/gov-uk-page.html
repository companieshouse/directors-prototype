Appoint, update or remove a director or company secretary

You can use WebFiling or file a Companies House form to appoint, update or remove a:

  • company secretary
  • company director who is a person
  • corporate director

What a corporate director is

This is a company, firm, or any other kind of corporate body that is appointed as the director of another company.

For example, COMPANY 1 LIMITED could be the corporate director of COMPANY 2 LIMITED.

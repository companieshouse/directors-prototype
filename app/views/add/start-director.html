Appoint a new director

{% from "govuk/components/inset-text/macro.njk" import govukInsetText %}

To appoint a new director, you'll need to tell us their:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • nationality
  • job title
  • home address and postal address
I do not want my home address to be publicly available
Your home address is not shown on the Companies House public register. However, if you use your home address as your postal address, this will be shown on the public register.
If you run your company from home and do not want your home address available to the public, there is a list of agents who can provide registered office services (opens in new tab).
A quick online search will show other providers who offer similar services. All providers will charge a fee.

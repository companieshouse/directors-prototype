How the director's home address is currently recorded

This address is currently recorded as the 'same as the correspondence address'. This means that any updates you make to the director's correspondence address also apply to their home address.

However, if you update the director's home address, it will no longer be recorded as the 'same'. This means that if you make updates to their correspondence address in the future, they will no longer apply to their home address as well.

If you need to update the director's home address, but want to keep the addresses recorded as the 'same', select 'no' and update their correspondence address instead.

How the director's correspondence address is currently recorded

This address is currently recorded as the 'same as the registered office address'. This means that any updates you make to the company's registered office address also apply to the director's correspondence address.

However, if you update the correspondence address, it will no longer be recorded as the 'same'. This means that if you make updates to the registered office address in the future, they will no longer apply to the correspondence address as well.

If you need to update the correspondence address, but want to keep the addresses recorded as the 'same', update the registered office address instead.

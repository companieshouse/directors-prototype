Correspondence address recorded as 'same as registered office address'

Any updates made to the company's registered office address currently also apply to the director's correspondence address.

If you update the correspondence address, it will no longer be recorded as the 'same as the registered office address'. This means that if you make updates to the registered office address in the future, they will no longer apply to the correspondence address.

If you want to update the correspondence address, and keep these addresses recorded as the 'same', change the registered office address instead (opens in new tab).

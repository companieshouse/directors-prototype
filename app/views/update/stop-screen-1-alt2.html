Correspondence address details

The director’s correspondence address is currently recorded as:

same as the registered office address

This means that updates you make to the company's registered office address also apply to the director's correspondence address.

However, if you update the correspondence address, it will no longer be recorded as the 'same'. This means that if you make updates to the registered office address in the future, they will no longer apply to the correspondence address as well.

Update the registered office address instead (opens in a new tab).
